About Me

Ever since I began my 12-year journey of digital marketing I have always been fascinated with the web development aspect of bringing digital assets to life. How was it that a developer can take a wireframe, graphic representation, and set of desired outcomes and bring it to life on the web? Over the years I had the benefit of working alongside some very talented development teams and got a peek into their world which only fueled my desire to figure out how the magic happened. When I moved to Nashville and started to meet some alumni of Nashville Software School that I began to think that web development could be something I could learn and make a new career out of. I went to an info session at NSS in August of 2017 because I was yearning for a change in pace and thought I'd see what all the hype was about. Afterward, I applied and was accepted to the full-time program for Feb. 2018. At the time I ended up accepting the position at Senior Market Advisors and had to postpone my acceptance. In early 2019 I found myself feeling unfulfilled in my position at Senior Market Advisors and decided to reach out to NSS to see if I could re-apply. Luckily I was able to gain acceptance & my journey has begun!
